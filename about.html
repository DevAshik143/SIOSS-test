ABOUT MORE

The Swedish International Organization of Sport Science (SiOSS) was established in Sweden in 2015. It is a specialized educational institution dedicated to the training and development of sports coaches across various disciplines, using the latest teaching methodologies and interactive technologies.

SiOSS is committed to transferring modern sports technology and knowledge to the Arab region, providing a high-quality educational environment that aligns with international standards in sports science and coaching. Its training programs cover performance science, sports analysis, rehabilitation, sports nutrition, and more.

The organization is led by Dr. Abdulazim Ahmed Al-Awsi, an experienced sports science expert with extensive expertise in both European and Arab sports education and training.

SiOSS offers internationally recognized certifications that can be notarized and authenticated by the Swedish Ministry of Foreign Affairs, making them valid for educational and professional use both within Sweden and abroad.

Through a broad network of experts and global partnerships, SiOSS plays a key role in advancing the sports coaching sector in the region and creating new opportunities for professionals interested in modern sports science.
